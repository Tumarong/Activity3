Philippines


With more than 7,000 islands, the Philippines takes pride in its local tourism and travel. There's always something going on: from the picturesque landscapes in Batanes to the pristine islands of Tawi-Tawi. At SPOT.ph's Things to Do section, we keep you up to date with the things to do in the Philippines and around the world. Find out what your neighborhood has to offer, such as a local park that deserves a visit or a nearby hang-out spot for you and your friends..

LUZON



Luzon is the country's (and 15th in the world's) largest island, roughly divided into four sections: Northern Luzon, Central Luzon, Southern Luzon, and the National Capital Region. These regions are accessible from Manila City by flights, and land vehicles like buses and private guided Luzon tours with transfers.

MINDANAO



Mindanao's largest city is Davao, which showcases the conservation area for the Philippine Eagle, the country's national bird. Davao is also home to the colorful Kadayawan Festival, one of the major festivals in the Philippines.
